Tips for Best Results
  • Use high-quality, clear PDFs/images
  • Ensure text is readable and not handwritten
  • For best OCR results, use scanned documents with at least 300 DPI
  • Break very long documents (100+ pages) into smaller chunks
  • Review generated questions carefully before publishing
